Boarding School

Introduction

Bal Jagat English Boarding School is an English medium school which was established in 2051 B.S.. It focuses to English as well as it regards the Nepali language as a National language of Nepal. English is the principal international language of diplomacy. Learning English is to open up the world for our children and youth. It gives students the ability to become active participants of the multilingual and multicultural world they live in. The school provides students with clear guidelines on what they are learning and how they will be assessed.

The students of Bal Jagat develop and consolidate the required knowledge and skills to meet the anticipated future demands of work and citizenship.

At last the image of school is to produce able , skillful and energetic citizen in the country.

Vision

Bal Jagat English Boarding School envisions being a nationally and internationally recognized, educationally recognized, educational institution for imparting quality education at an affordable fees.
